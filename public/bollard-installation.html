Bollard Installation / Signage
Protect Your Property

Commercial asphalt services done right — on time, on budget, nationwide.

Cheap Insurance, Big Protection

Bollards do two things: protect your building from vehicle impacts and define traffic flow in your parking area. A truck taking out a storefront corner is a six-figure problem. Bollards are cheap insurance.

A1 installs steel pipe bollards filled with concrete — the commercial standard. We core drill the existing asphalt or concrete, set the bollard in a poured concrete footing, and finish the surface clean. Standard colors are safety yellow, but we do custom colors to match your brand standards.

We've installed bollards at Amazon fulfillment centers, retail storefronts, loading docks, and drive-throughs.

  • Steel pipe bollards filled with concrete
  • Core-drilled into existing asphalt or concrete
  • Standard safety yellow or custom brand colors
  • Storefront protection and traffic control
  • Loading docks, drive-throughs, entryways

Removable Bollards

Need access for deliveries or seasonal events? We also install removable bollard systems — the post stays in the ground, the bollard lifts out with a key.

Great for loading areas that need to be open during business hours and secured after.
