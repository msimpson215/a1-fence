Axon Email Orb

Your email can have a conversation with you — without touching Gmail. The orb in the email is the table; the popup is the plate floating on top.

Gmail = the table. You can only put images and links in the email.
Axon = the plate. A small chromeless window opens on top of Gmail with a transparent edge so the orb looks like it belongs in the message. The AI runs on our servers (ChatGPT-powered), never inside Google.

Best illusion — JavaScript hyperlink on the image

Paste this as the link URL when you attach a hyperlink to the orb image in Gmail. Gmail stays open; a small window opens centered on the reader’s screen.

Some clients strip javascript: links. If that happens, use the HTTPS option below and accept a new tab (weaker illusion) or ask recipients to open in Gmail web.

Fallback — HTTPS link (works everywhere)

Use this URL on the orb image or on a text button like “Talk with our AI.” Opens the same orb UI; may open a tab instead of floating over Gmail.

Email body HTML (copy into Gmail)

  1. In Gmail, compose your message and insert your orb image (centered).
  2. Select the image → Link → paste the JavaScript or HTTPS URL above.
  3. Send. When they click, the plate (Axon popup) appears over the table (Gmail).
