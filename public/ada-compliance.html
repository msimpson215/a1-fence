Don't Wait for a Complaint

ADA violations are expensive. A single complaint can trigger an investigation, and non-compliant parking lots are one of the most common targets. Getting compliant is straightforward if you work with someone who knows the standards.

A1 handles full ADA compliance packages — handicap space sizing and placement, van-accessible spaces, access aisles, curb ramps, signage, and surface slopes. We know what inspectors look for.

We've done ADA compliance work for Sam's Club, Lowe's, CVS, and dozens of national clients. We understand the federal requirements and the specific interpretations that come up in the field.

  • Handicap space sizing and placement
  • Van-accessible space requirements
  • Access aisle striping and markings
  • Curb ramp installation and repair
  • Signage and surface slope compliance
