What Is an AI Estimate?

We use artificial intelligence to analyze a video of your property perimeter — identifying potholes, cracks, fading, and surface damage. AI calculates your lot size via satellite imagery. Once that’s done, one of our asphalt professionals reviews everything, makes sure it’s accurate, and emails you a detailed estimate.

Here’s How It Works

  • 1.Schedule a quick call. One of our team members calls you at your chosen time.
  • 2.Walk your property perimeter with your smartphone camera on. Our team member sees exactly what you see — live. They’ll guide you: “Can you zoom in there? Okay, now pull back so I can see the full lot.”
  • 3.Our AI analyzes the video — every pothole, crack, and worn area is identified. Lot size is calculated via satellite. No tape measure needed.
  • 4.Within 15 minutes, you get a professional estimate emailed to you — with a cover sheet showing exactly what was found, photos with arrows pointing to problem areas, and a full cost breakdown.

No site visit required. No waiting days for a bid.
