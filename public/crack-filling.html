Crack Filling
Stop It Early

Commercial asphalt services done right — on time, on budget, nationwide.

Small Cracks, Big Problems

Small cracks turn into big problems fast. Water gets in, the freeze-thaw cycle does its work, and what started as a hairline crack becomes a pothole that needs full-depth patching. I've seen it happen in a single winter.

We use CrafCo hot-applied rubberized crack filler — the commercial standard. It expands and contracts with temperature changes, which means it stays sealed season after season. We clean the crack, dry it, and apply under heat for maximum adhesion.

Crack filling is almost always the first step before sealcoating. You don't put a new coat over open cracks — you seal the lot from the ground up.

  • Hot-applied CrafCo rubberized filler
  • Expands and contracts with temperature changes
  • Highway, commercial, and industrial applications
  • Prevents water infiltration and sub-base damage
  • Always recommended before sealcoating

Highway Grade Work

A1 handles crack filling for municipalities, state highway contracts, and national retail chains. We have the equipment and crew to handle large-scale linear footage efficiently.

We also do mastic applications for airports and high-traffic roadways where standard crack filler isn't enough.
