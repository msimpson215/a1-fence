Asphalt Patching

A1 Professional Asphalt and Sealing can assist with your parking lot concerns and prevent any future liability issues without major impact to customers and employees.

Problem: Potholes and Broken Areas

Potholes create serious liability issues in parking lot and roadway areas due to the potential hazard to vehicles and pedestrians. Repairing potholes before liability issues arise can avoid frivolous lawsuits and unreasonable injury settlements. Potholes can form during any season and should be repaired immediately before causing serious injury or damage.

Solution: Asphalt Patching

Potholes and deteriorated areas in asphalt parking lots can quickly expand and become very costly without the appropriate attention. To prevent this from happening, we offer patching of various sized areas. This entails milling out a minimum depth of 2 inches to full depth of asphalt. Area is then replaced with a hot mix asphalt that is rolled in to ensure compaction.

  • Potholes are caused from water getting into cracks in the asphalt and reaching the base
  • The moisture then weakens your rock base — asphalt is only as strong as what lies beneath it
  • If not repaired correctly, the depth of destruction may require removing the rock base as well
  • Deteriorated areas caused by heavy traffic, trash trucks, delivery vehicles and semi-trucks are repaired the same way

Crack Filling

Crack Filling is a major aspect of maintaining the integrity of your parking lot. Crack Filling is the application of a hot rubberized filler in the major cracks in your parking lot. We apply this with industry leading equipment in which the rubber has been melted to meet parking lot specifications.

  • If water is allowed to enter a crack, it will continue to the base, eventually eroding the asphalt and causing deterioration of the asphalt or worse, a pothole
  • Crack Filling on a regular basis plays a major part in the prevention of such occurrence
  • Hot rubber is utilized for the purpose of expansion and contraction — it completely seals the sides of the crack not allowing water penetration or the crack to further expand
