Welcome to A1 Professional Asphalt & Sealing, LLC

A1 Professional Asphalt & Sealing, LLC takes pride in our work and the relationships we build with every client. We are a full-service commercial asphalt company serving St. Louis, Missouri and clients across 17 states. We use only industry-leading products — SealMaster, Sherwin Williams, and CrafCo — because quality materials produce quality results.

Every project we take on gets the same commitment: show up on time, do it right, and stand behind the work. From small parking lot sealcoating jobs to full-scale milling and repaving contracts, A1 delivers the same standard on every site.
