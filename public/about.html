Our Story

Built on Showing Up & Doing It Right

A1 Professional Asphalt & Sealing was founded in 2014 by Joe Schanz with a simple belief — if you show up on time, do quality work, and treat every customer like family, the business will take care of itself.

What started as a one-truck operation in the St. Louis metro has grown into a full-service commercial asphalt company operating across 17 states with 4 offices and a crew that takes real pride in their work.

We specialize in large commercial properties — parking lots, distribution centers, municipalities, churches, and strip malls. Our clients include national retail brands, local municipalities, and everything in between.

Joe is still hands-on, still on job sites, and still the person who picks up the phone. That's not going to change.
